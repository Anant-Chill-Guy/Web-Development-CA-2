About the Club

The Innovators Club is a student-run organization that aims to foster creativity, technical skills, and teamwork. Founded in 2020, our club organizes workshops, speaker sessions, hackathons, and project labs to provide hands-on learning experiences.

We welcome students from all fields whether you are into coding, design, or entrepreneurship. Our mission is to provide a supportive environment where members can experiment, fail safely without any fear, and ultimately succeed.

Members gain practical skills, mentorship, and opportunities to present projects at college and higher level events. Join us to find collaborators and mentors for your next idea!
